Your Guide to Essential Nutrients and Healthy Eating

What is a Balanced Diet?

A balanced diet includes a variety of foods in the right proportions to provide the nutrients your body needs. These nutrients include carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, minerals, fiber, and water.

Tip: No single food provides all the nutrients your body needs. Incorporate a mix of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats into your meals.

Tips for Creating a Balanced Diet

  • Include a variety of foods.
  • Focus on whole, unprocessed foods.
  • Prioritize fruits and vegetables.
  • Choose lean proteins.
  • Incorporate healthy fats.
  • Limit added sugars and processed foods.
  • Stay hydrated.
  • Practice portion control.
  • Plan your meals.
  • Listen to your body.

Sample Balanced Meal Plan

Breakfast

Greek yogurt with berries, nuts, and honey.

Lunch

Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens and olive oil dressing.

Snack

Apple slices with almond butter.

Dinner

Baked salmon, quinoa, and steamed broccoli.

Why a Balanced Diet Matters

A balanced diet is key to maintaining energy levels, supporting mental clarity, and reducing the risk of chronic diseases like heart disease, diabetes, and obesity.
